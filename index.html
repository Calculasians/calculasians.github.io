Who am I

Hi, or 大家好! I am currently attending UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering & Computer Science.

Within the academic sphere, my Berkeley education has been characterized by a distinct inability to decide which specialty I want to delve into. The consequence is that I have taken a wide range of classes in Security, Parallel Programs, Digital Logic Design, and of course, the holy grail of Algorithms. Right now, my research and work experience center around FPGA/ASIC design, but who knows where the wind blows me in the future?

Outside of the academic sphere, I may also be found playing frisbee or badminton, catching up on a variety of Netflix shows, or on top of a mountain. I enjoy exploring interesting places & scenery in the wilderness, and I have been to many national parks across the western US, with many more to go. I also have a list of 20 or so languages I would like to learn in the future, so I may also be caught brushing up on my Turkish, or inventing a new language altogether.
