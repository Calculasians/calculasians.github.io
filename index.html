Who am I

Hi, or 大家好! I am currently attending UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering & Computer Science. My interests lie in a wide variety of topics, including embedded systems & AI, and their applications in areas ranging from robotics to renewable energy.

Outside of the academic sphere, I may also be found playing badminton, catching up on a variety of Netflix shows, or on top of a mountain. I enjoy exploring interesting places & scenery in the wilderness, and I have been to 30 national parks across the western US, with many more to go.

Lastly, one of my greatest personal hobbies is foreign language learning. As a child, I was fascinated by the "polyglots" I would see on Youtube. I have dabbled in quite a few languages in the past, as well as having self-studied to a considerable level, Japanese, Spanish, and my favorite language, Polish. I am interested in how languages evolved over time, as well as exciting hobbies such as creating exotic conlangs.
